Welcome to Krish's Arithmatic Hall.

You will be given 90 seconds to solve as many arithmatic problems as you can. You will be asked to compute:

  • x + y where x and y are numbers between 2 and 100
  • x - y where x > y (addition in reverse)
  • ab where a is between 2 and 12 and b is between 2 and 100
  • a/b where b divides a (multiplication in reverse)

This means every answer will be nonegative integers. The website will record your score and the time you took the assesment to track your progress. [IN DEVELOPMENT] Good luck!
